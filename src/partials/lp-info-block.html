Black is the Color

Founded in 2018, this brand new vocal group is focused on performing original and traditional songs from the Canadian Maritimes, in both traditional and styles.

The group was created to perform songs and ballads otherwise overlooked by traditional pub musicians. Connoisseurs of traditional music, Black is the Colour has painstakingly written or selected every song on the playlist based on merit, (not popularity!). This is the music that musicians listen to, nary a Black Velvet Band to be seen!
